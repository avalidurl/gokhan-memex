# NVIDIA vs Total Crypto Market Cap Dashboard

An interactive financial dashboard that provides real-time comparison between NVIDIA's market capitalization and the total cryptocurrency market cap. This tool enables instant market analysis and helps track the relationship between traditional tech stocks and the crypto ecosystem.

## Features

- **Real-time Data**: Live updates of NVIDIA stock price and total crypto market cap
- **Interactive Visualization**: Clean, responsive charts for easy comparison
- **Market Analysis**: Side-by-side comparison of two major market segments
- **Financial Metrics**: Detailed breakdown of market capitalizations
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile viewing

## Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies and deployed on Vercel for optimal performance and reliability.

## Use Cases

- Investment research and analysis
- Market trend monitoring
- Educational tool for understanding market relationships
- Quick reference for financial professionals and enthusiasts
