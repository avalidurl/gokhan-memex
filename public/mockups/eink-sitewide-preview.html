Why this feels more readable

The baseline uses a warm off-white background and deep inky text rather than cool gray or pure black/white contrast. The result is lower visual harshness while preserving legibility over long sessions.

Paragraph size scales fluidly with clamp(), maintaining a consistent measure near 65 characters per line. Spacing is bottom-biased to avoid doubled paragraph gaps.

E-ink style is not nostalgia. It is a practical way to reduce visual fatigue and keep attention on words.

Reduced motion by default rules

Smooth scrolling and transitions are constrained to motion-safe contexts. For reduced-motion users, interactions are immediate and predictable with no decorative animation overhead.

Route-level consistency

This mockup shows a single token and typography system applied to homepage cards, archive scanning blocks, and long-form article reading surfaces. It demonstrates the site-wide target before implementation.
